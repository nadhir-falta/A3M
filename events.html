2017 A3M Summer Picnic

The Algerian American Association of Michigan (A3M) is inviting all our community members for a summer picnic on July 16. We truly hope you could join us for this special day of fun, food, activities, and entertainment. Please see below details of the 2017 A3M Summer Picnic:


What: A3M Summer Picnic
When: Sunday, July 16, 2017 from 2-9 pm
Where: Kensington Park
Who is the sponsor: A3M Steering Committee and friends
What to bring: Sweets, Coffee, Tea or fruits to share with friends.

Please confirm your attendance here or by contacting Slim Benloucif via email or WhatsApp.


Thank you,
Slim on Behalf of the A3M Steering Committee
