What We Do

The Algerian American Association Of Michigan (A3M) was founded in 2016 with the following goals:


  • To provide a forum for Algerian Americans to serve their interests and preserve their rich cultural traditions so they and their children would take pride in their unique heritage, their knowledge of Multiple languages, and their bicultural experience.

  • To promote understanding and enhance interaction between the Algerian-American community and the rest of the American public and its institutions.

  • To promote understanding and cooperation between the American and Algerian peoples, and support Algeria’s efforts toward progress. A3M is planning to hold several annual functions, which include but are not limited to Major Holiday events such as the “ Mutual US-DZ Independence Day Celebration ”, " EIDs", and other social and recreational events which attract adults, youth and children.


A3M is attempting to obtain a nonprofit tax-exempt Michigan corporation status, required documentation has been filed with proper authorities and upon receipt of an approval, we will communicate to our community such information.


A3M relies on the volunteer work of its members, who are professionals that offer their time, effort and resources to help achieve the goals that the organization is trying to accomplish.


The success or failure of A3M is ultimately linked to the participation of its members. The Actual Steering Committee and The Actual Board Directors are nothing more than active volunteer members committed for a set period of time to fulfill a minimum program. It is the A3M future members who should animate activities with the help of the Board.


The A3M’s bylaws allow complementing classes of membership for Algerian Americans and others who are interested in the Algerian culture and the A3M activities.

For further information regarding A3M, please contact us via email: ﻿﻿﻿﻿info@A3Michigan.org
