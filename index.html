Announcement:

Assalamu Alaikum,

Dear community member,


We are embarking on a very exciting program for our Algerian youth by forming a committee that will listen to them, involve them, and empower them.


Listen • Involve • Empower


This youth committee will bring together high school age, college age, and young professionals closer to exchange ideas, benefit each other, and nurture their unique Algerian American identity.


This A3M committee is spearheaded by the following people:

  • Mohamed Maamir
  • Mohamed Boukhatmi
  • Abdel-Aziz Abada
  • Maream Boukhatmi

Please reach out to the committee members above with your interest as they wish to organize their first event to brainstorm ideas on how to best create value for our youth.
First meeting is tentatively scheduled on October 7th.

A3M PROGRAMS AND SERVICES

To take full advantage of the A3M programs and services please register. Instructions, requirements and applications for the programs and services available, are listed on the secured profile area of the website (accessible once registered).

Scholarships

Scholarship program for our hard working youth, designed to encourage and support them to achieve their goals.

Legal Representation

Legal Representation for the community available in multiple areas of the American law.


Mentorship Program

Realizing that our community has a lot to offer, we are in the process of setting up a mentorship program that would connect mentees to mentors in hopefully various skills.

Driving Classes

Partnership with an accommodating driving school for those in need of driving lessons (Cars, Buses and Trucks)
